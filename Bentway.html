BENTWAY

Under The Highway

For a brand new outdoor public event space, The Bentway has a lot of history. It runs under Toronto’s Gardiner Expressway, which was built in 1958 and has been cutting the city off from it’s waterfront ever since. We leaned into that legacy to turn their launch into a unifying celebration for the entire city — a block party 60 years in the making.
